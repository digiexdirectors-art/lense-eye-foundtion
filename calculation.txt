= (Completed Appointments ÷ Total Appointments) × 100%
A completed appointment means the patient was seen and attended — which acts as a proxy for satisfaction. Cancelled/no-show appointments bring this down. The backend floors it at 90% so new clinics never show a low number.


If you want a more accurate satisfaction metric in future, you could add a patient feedback form (star rating after appointment) and average those scores. The backend is already structured to accept a satisfactionRate field override if you want to feed it from a feedback collection.
